About Us

BloomsField offers a variety of extra curriculur activities for the kids Bloomsfield School is a Pre-Primary School(Day Care, Baby Class,Nursery and Pre-Unit) preparing the children for both the 8-4-4 and International curriculums. The staff members comprise of fully trained and experienced Teachers in Early childhood Education(ECE). The Head Teacher has a valuable and extensive experience in the 8-4-4 carriculum, the British and Montessori systems, having worked in leading International Schools in the country. We provide holistic education in a conductive environment that develops the children's physical, mental, social and spiritual faculties. We provide an environment in the important early years where children truly love to learn and learn to love. We enhance and grow talent and creativity at an early age using modern teaching methods. We inculcate high moral values that will enrich the child to grow with self esteem, integrity, respect and demeanor.

Our Vision

"At Bloomsfield Kindergarten, our vision statement is ‘To be the school of choice and leading perfomer in providing quality education based on christian principles.’. We value all staff and children as individuals and aim to provide a safe, caring and happy environment where all our children develop the confidence to thrive. We put emphasis on building successful relationships with parents and carers, to enable us to support each child’s learning and development. Above all, we believe learning should be enjoyable for everyone involved."
